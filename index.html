Recent projects that I'm particularly proud of:

Now their entire staff is able to reliably focus on their work, instead of being constantly stymied by IT issues like they were before they came to me.
  • An SMS-XMPP (Jabber) gateway on Google Compute Engine + Twilio, with tie-ins to Helpscout and Highrise, so as to provide customer support with multiple customer support agents presenting a cohesive and unified customer-facing interface at a cost of almost $0/month.

Used Linux with a custom initrd, clamscan (set to be very sensitive) for initial local scanning, and submission to Google's VirusTotal API for verification/refutation of malware detection results. Included some filtering (specific to the customer) to avoid sending sensitive data to Google.
